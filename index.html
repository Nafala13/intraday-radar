Technical Indicators

EMA — Exponential Moving Average

Periods 20 50 90 calculated across all three timeframes (4H, 1H, 15m). Used to determine trend direction, stack alignment, and slope momentum. Bullish condition requires: Price > EMA20 > EMA50 > EMA90.

ADX — Average Directional Index period 14

Measures trend strength, not direction. Hard filter: ADX must exceed 20 on both 4H and 1H timeframes. Ensures signals only appear during trending conditions — not sideways/ranging markets.

RSI — Relative Strength Index period 14

Measured on 15m. Filters signals to neutral zone only: 40–65 for bullish, 35–60 for bearish. Prevents entries during overbought or oversold conditions.

MACD 12 · 26 · 9

Measured on 15m. Uses the MACD Histogram (MACD line minus signal line) to confirm momentum direction. Positive histogram required for bullish signals, negative for bearish.

Volume Spike

Compares the last candle's volume against the 20-candle rolling average. A spike is detected when current volume exceeds 1.5× the average — confirming that price movement is backed by real activity.

Candlestick Pattern Classifier

Automatically classifies the latest 15m candle into one of 8 types based on body ratio and wick structure:

STRONG_BULL REJECTION_BULL NORMAL_BULL STRONG_BEAR REJECTION_BEAR NORMAL_BEAR DOJI NO_CONFIRM

Swing Low / High SL lookback 10 candles

Stop loss is auto-calculated from structural swing points. Bullish SL = lowest low of last 10 candles. Bearish SL = highest high of last 10 candles. Risk is capped between 0.6% and 2.5% of entry price.

Smart Filters

🔍 BTC Trend Check (4H)

Before scanning any altcoin, the bot checks BTC's 4H condition. If BTC is bearish (price below EMA20 below EMA50, ADX > 20), all bullish signals are automatically skipped. Prevents long entries against the macro trend.

BULL — scan normally NEUTRAL — scan with caution BEAR — long signals skipped

📊 Open Interest Divergence Filter

Uses Binance Futures API to compare OI change vs price movement over the last 3 periods (15m). If price is rising but OI is falling (short covering rally, not real buying), the signal is automatically rejected. Coins without a futures market are not penalized.

CONFIRM — real buying pressure NEUTRAL — inconclusive, signal passes DIVERGE — auto-skipped NO_FUTURES — passes, not penalized

📍 EMA20 Proximity Filter

Entry is only valid if price is within 0.4% of the 15m EMA20. This ensures the bot catches pullback entries rather than chasing price after a move has already happened.

Scoring System — 9 Conditions
# Condition Timeframe Points
01 4H EMA Stack (20 > 50 > 90) 4H +1
02 4H EMA50 Slope (trending direction) 4H +1
03 1H EMA Alignment (price vs EMA20 vs EMA50) 1H +1
04 ADX > 20 on both 4H and 1H 4H + 1H +1
05 RSI in neutral zone 15m +1
06 MACD histogram confirms direction 15m +1
07 Volume spike (>1.5× 20-candle avg) 15m +1
08 Candlestick confirmation pattern 15m +1
09 OI Confirm (no divergence) Futures +1

Minimum score to emit a signal: 5/9. Recommended entry threshold: 7–9/9.

Recommended Entry Flow
01
Wait for a signal with score 7+/9 — lower scores have too many weak conditions and are not recommended for live entries.
02
Check OI Status = CONFIRM — this means real buying pressure is behind the move, not just short covering.
03
Verify BTC is BULL or NEUTRAL — never enter a long position when BTC is in a confirmed downtrend.
04
Open the chart manually — confirm price is near a pullback zone (EMA20/EMA50 on 1H or 4H), not extended from a recent pump.
05
Enter at the signal's entry price — use the bot's calculated SL and TP directly. Do not move the SL after entering.
